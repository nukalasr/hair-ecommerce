Refund & Return Policy

Last Updated: {{ lastUpdated }}

1. Overview

At Hair Bundles Ecommerce, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a flexible return and refund policy designed to ensure your satisfaction while maintaining product quality standards.

This policy outlines the conditions under which returns and refunds are accepted. Please read carefully before making a purchase.

2. Return Window

You may return eligible products within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

  • Days 1-30: Full refund or exchange available
  • After 30 days: Returns not accepted (see exceptions below)

Important: The 30-day period begins on the date of delivery, not the date of purchase.

3. Eligibility for Returns

3.1 Returnable Products

To be eligible for a return, products must:

  • Be unopened and unused
  • Be in original packaging with all tags attached
  • Include all accessories and documentation
  • Be in resellable condition
  • Have proof of purchase (order number or receipt)

3.2 Non-Returnable Products

The following items cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons:

  • Opened hair bundles or wigs
  • Custom-colored or specially ordered items
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable products
  • Products marked "non-returnable" at purchase

3.3 Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • Include your order number
  • We will provide a prepaid return label
  • Replacement or full refund will be issued

4. Return Process

Step 1: Initiate Return

To start a return:

  • Log into your account
  • Go to "Order History"
  • Select the order and click "Request Return"
  • Provide reason for return
  • Submit return request

Alternative: Email support@hairbundles.com with your order number and return reason.

Step 2: Receive Authorization

Within 24-48 hours, you will receive:

  • Return authorization number (RMA)
  • Return shipping label (if eligible)
  • Packing instructions

Note: Do not ship items back without an RMA number. Unauthorized returns will not be processed.

Step 3: Package and Ship

  • Pack items securely in original packaging
  • Include RMA number inside the package
  • Attach return label to outside of package
  • Ship via provided method
  • Keep tracking number for your records

Step 4: Return Inspection

Upon receiving your return, we will:

  • Inspect the product within 3-5 business days
  • Verify it meets return eligibility criteria
  • Send email notification of inspection results

Step 5: Refund Processing

If approved:

  • Refund processed within 7-10 business days
  • Funds returned to original payment method
  • Email confirmation sent

5. Refund Methods

5.1 Original Payment Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 7-14 business days

Note: Processing times may vary by financial institution. The refund appears as "HAIR BUNDLES REFUND" on your statement.

5.2 Store Credit

Alternatively, you may choose store credit for faster processing:

  • Instant credit to your account
  • 10% bonus credit (e.g., $100 return = $110 credit)
  • No expiration date
  • Can be used on future purchases

5.3 Exchanges

For exchanges (different size, color, or product):

  • Same price item: No additional charge
  • Higher price item: Pay the difference
  • Lower price item: Receive store credit for difference
  • Shipping fees may apply

6. Shipping Costs

6.1 Return Shipping

Return shipping costs depend on the reason for return:

  • Changed Mind: Customer pays return shipping
  • Wrong Item Sent: We provide prepaid label
  • Defective Product: We provide prepaid label
  • Damaged in Transit: We provide prepaid label

6.2 Shipping Cost Deductions

If you received free shipping on your original order, the shipping cost ($9.99 domestic, $29.99 international) will be deducted from your refund.

6.3 Exchange Shipping

  • Return shipping: Customer pays (unless defective)
  • Outbound shipping for exchange: Free

7. Refund Amount

7.1 What's Included

Your refund includes:

  • Product purchase price
  • Sales tax (if applicable)

7.2 What's Not Included

The following are NOT refunded:

  • Original shipping costs (unless error on our part)
  • Return shipping costs
  • Customs fees or import duties
  • Gift wrapping fees

7.3 Partial Returns

If you return part of an order:

  • Refund calculated based on items returned
  • Promotional discounts may be adjusted
  • Example: Buy 2 Get 1 Free - returning 1 item may affect discount

8. Special Circumstances

8.1 Defective Products

If you receive a defective product:

  • Full refund including shipping costs
  • Prepaid return label provided
  • Option for replacement at no cost
  • Extended return window (60 days)

8.2 Wrong Item Received

If we sent the wrong item:

  • Keep the incorrect item while we send the correct one
  • Prepaid return label provided
  • No return shipping cost
  • Expedited replacement shipping

8.3 Missing Items

If your order is missing items:

  • Contact us within 7 days
  • We'll ship missing items at no charge
  • Expedited shipping if our error

8.4 Lost or Stolen Packages

For lost or stolen packages:

  • Report within 7 days of expected delivery
  • We'll investigate with the carrier
  • Replacement or refund issued if unrecovered
  • Not responsible if signature was required and obtained

9. International Returns

9.1 Return Process

International returns follow the same process but note:

  • Extended return window (45 days)
  • Customer pays return shipping unless defective
  • Must ship via trackable method
  • Customs forms required

9.2 Customs and Duties

  • Original customs fees are non-refundable
  • We are not responsible for return customs charges
  • Mark return packages as "Returned Merchandise"

9.3 Refund Currency

Refunds issued in original purchase currency. Exchange rate fluctuations may result in different amounts.

10. Refused or Rejected Returns

Returns may be rejected if:

  • Product has been used or worn
  • Packaging or tags removed
  • Item damaged by customer
  • Return window expired
  • No RMA number provided
  • Hygiene seal broken (hair products)

Rejected returns will be shipped back to you at your expense, or you may choose to donate the item.

11. Cancellations

11.1 Before Shipping

Orders can be cancelled free of charge if:

  • Requested within 2 hours of purchase
  • Order has not been shipped
  • Contact customer support immediately

11.2 After Shipping

Once shipped, follow standard return process upon delivery.

11.3 Seller Cancellations

We reserve the right to cancel orders for:

  • Pricing errors
  • Product unavailability
  • Fraudulent transactions
  • Address verification issues

Full refund issued within 3-5 business days for cancelled orders.

12. Refund Delays

Contact us if you haven't received your refund after the stated timeframe. Delays may occur due to:

  • Bank processing times
  • Payment provider holds
  • Credit card billing cycles
  • Holidays or weekends

We will provide a refund reference number for tracking purposes.
